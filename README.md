## 🇧🇷 Apresentação do Projeto

### O que é o LouvorHub?

Se você já participou de um culto ou evento religioso, sabe o quanto de trabalho invisível acontece nos bastidores: escolher as músicas da semana, avisar os músicos, confirmar quem vai tocar, definir a ordem das canções e ainda garantir que o pastor aprove tudo a tempo.

**O LouvorHub resolve exatamente isso.**

É uma plataforma digital completa para ministérios de louvor e igrejas, que transforma um processo cheio de mensagens no WhatsApp, planilhas do Excel e esquecimentos em algo simples, organizado e profissional.

---

### 💡 Como funciona na prática?

Imagine o seguinte cenário de uma semana típica numa igreja:

1. **O ministro monta o repertório do culto** — escolhe as músicas, organiza a ordem, define em qual tom cada uma será cantada e até ajusta o tom para a voz feminina ou masculina.

2. **Os músicos são convocados automaticamente** — o sistema avisa quem vai tocar, em qual instrumento e quais músicas cada um é responsável.

3. **Cada músico confirma se conhece as músicas** — pelo próprio sistema, em vez de uma thread interminável no WhatsApp.

4. **O pastor revisa e aprova o repertório** — com um clique, sem precisar de reunião.

5. **Todo mundo fica sincronizado** — notificações em tempo real sobre alterações, lembretes do culto e avisos importantes.

O LouvorHub é uma aplicação web **completa**, com:

## 🇺🇸 English Version

<a name="english-version"></a>

### What is LouvorHub?

If you've ever attended a church service, you know how much invisible work happens behind the scenes: picking the week's songs, notifying musicians, confirming who will play, setting the order of worship, and making sure the pastor approves everything on time.

**LouvorHub solves exactly that.**

It's a complete digital platform for worship ministries and churches, turning a process full of WhatsApp messages, Excel spreadsheets, and missed communications into something simple, organized, and professional.

---

### 💡 How does it work in practice?

Picture a typical week at a church:

1. **The worship leader builds the service setlist** — picks songs, sets the order, chooses the musical key for each one, and adjusts it for male or female vocal range.

2. **Musicians are automatically notified** — the system tells each musician which songs they're responsible for and with which instrument.

3. **Each musician confirms they know the songs** — directly in the system, instead of an endless WhatsApp thread.

4. **The pastor reviews and approves the setlist** — with a single click, no meeting required.

5. **Everyone stays in sync** — real-time notifications for changes, service reminders, and important announcements.

---

### ✅ Key Features

| Feature | Who it's for |
|---------|--------------|
| 📋 Setlist builder with blocks (Opening, Worship, Offering, Solo...) | Worship leaders |
| 🎵 Song catalog with keys, BPM, YouTube & Spotify links | Entire team |
| 👥 Musician scheduling with per-song instrument assignment | Worship leaders |
| ✅ Song confirmation by each musician | Musicians & singers |
| 🔔 Automatic notifications and service reminders | Entire team |
| ✔️ Setlist approval workflow for the pastor | Pastors |
| ⛪ Member, church, and service type management | Administrators |
| 📊 Personal participation statistics dashboard | Everyone |
| 🗓️ Service calendar | Everyone |

---

### 🔒 Secure, Simple, and Accessible

- Every member logs in with **email and password**, with email verification
- Each person sees only what's relevant to their **role** (pastor, worship leader, musician, singer)
- Works on any device — desktop, phone, or tablet
- **No installation required** — everything runs in the browser

---

### 🏗️ What Was Built

LouvorHub is a **complete** web application, comprising:

- **Backend**: Robust API with 50+ endpoints, JWT authentication, email sending, role-based access control, and automated notification scheduling
- **Frontend**: Modern, responsive interface with 17 screens, built in Angular 19
- **Database**: Optimized relational structure with 15 tables in PostgreSQL (Supabase)

---

### 🎯 Who Is It For?

- Churches of any size looking to professionalize their worship ministry
- Ministries struggling with poor communication and disorganization
- Leaders who spend hours coordinating musicians over WhatsApp
- Pastors who want visibility and control over worship setlists
